Baldur's Gate: Upper City Intrigue

Five Short, Sharp Adventures from Beyond the Old Wall.

For use with the 2024 revision of the Dungeons & Dragons fifth edition core rules

Easy-to-prepare adventures, set against the ideal backdrop for schemes and deceit:

  • "Joust of the Oathbreaker" (level 3) Compete in a jousting tournament on the Wide against a wicked orc knight riding a giant lizard.
  • "Masque Macabre" (level 4) Infiltrate a decadent masquerade among wealthy Bhaal cultists at Ravenshade Manor, hosted by a murderous vampire patriar.
  • "The Wonderstone Job" (level 5) Pull off a heist under the Hall of Wonders in a vault full of mechanical traps and clockwork monsters.
  • "Tower of Chaos" (level 6) Tame a tumultuous storm of wild magic that has engulfed Ramazith's Tower and uncover its alien origin.
  • "High Stakes" (level 7) Play politics in High Hall among reptilian spies and parliamentary mummies as you elect the next duke to the Council of Four.

Price: $4.95
